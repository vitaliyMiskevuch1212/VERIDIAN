# 🌐 VERIDIAN: System Initialization & User Tutorial

Welcome to the **Veridian Intelligence Platform**! If you are new to geopolitical dashboards, the interface might look like something out of a sci-fi movie. Don't worry—this tutorial will break down everything you need to know to navigate the platform like a seasoned intelligence analyst.

---

## 1. What is Veridian?
Veridian is a **Real-Time Geopolitical Intelligence & AI Trading Signal** platform. It constantly scans global events (news, earthquakes, military flights, cyber threats) and uses Artificial Intelligence to tell you **how those events might impact the financial markets** (stocks, oil, gold, crypto, etc.).

---

## 2. Navigating the Command Center

When you first open the app, you will see three main sections:

### ⬅️ The Left Panel: Intelligence Feeds
*   **Smart Digest:** Your AI-generated daily briefing. This gives you a quick 3-bullet summary of the most critical global events happening *right now*.
*   **Pulse Feed:** A scrolling list of real-time events. It detects natural disasters, political protests, and breaking conflicts. 
*   **Analytical Search:** Use this text box to type in a specific country (e.g., "China" or "Germany") to instantly zoom the globe to that location and pull a dedicated intelligence brief.

### 🌍 The Center Stage: The Tactical Globe
*   **The 3D Globe:** This is your primary visualization tool. You can click and drag to spin the earth, and scroll your mouse wheel to zoom in and out.
*   **Hotspots (Red/Orange Glowing Circles):** These represent active conflicts, disasters, or high-tension zones anywhere on earth.
*   **Top Control Bar (Map Layers):** You'll see small icons directly above the globe. You can click these to toggle different data layers on/off (e.g., toggle military flights, maritime vessels, or switch to a flat 2D map).
*   **Bottom Console:** Here you can cycle through Military Aircraft tracked globally or view live Cyber Threat metrics.

### ➡️ The Right Panel: Financial Impact
*   **Market Watch:** Live prices of major assets (S&P 500, Gold, Crude Oil, Bitcoin).
*   **Top Keywords:** The AI's analysis of what words are trending in global intelligence right now.
*   **Top Navigation Buttons (Intel, Trade, Forecast, Sitrep, Signals):** Click these to open specialized modal windows for deep-dive analytics.

## 3. Core Features: How to Use Them

### 🚀 Feature 1: Get an AI Country Brief
1. Look at the **Left Panel**.
2. Type a country name into the **Analytical Search** bar (e.g., "Taiwan").
3. The globe will automatically spin to that country, and an AI intelligence brief will slide out, detailing the current political climate, tensions, and economic data for that region.

### 💹 Feature 2: Generate a GeoTrade Signal
Want to know if you should buy or sell a stock based on current news?
1. Click the **SIGNALS** button on the top right navigation menu.
2. In the panel that opens, enter a stock ticker (like `AAPL` for Apple, or `XOM` for ExxonMobil).
3. The AI will look at all the world's breaking news and generate a "Trade Signal" telling you if geopolitical events are currently **Bullish** (good) or **Bearish** (bad) for that asset, along with a confidence rating.

### 🎲 Feature 3: Run a Wargame Simulation
Wondering what would happen to oil prices if the Suez Canal closed?
1. Click the **FORECAST** button on the top right.
2. Under "Wargaming", type in a hypothetical scenario (e.g., *"Major cyberattack takes down US banking infrastructure"*). 
3. Click "Simulate". The AI will generate a detailed timeline of events, predict exactly which financial markets will crash, and which assets will act as a safe haven.

### ✈️ Feature 4: Live Military Flight Tracking
1. Look at the bottom center console beneath the globe.
2. Click on the **Military Aircraft** tab.
3. The globe will populate with small airplane icons. These are **real, live military aircraft** currently flying around the world. The platform intercepts their open transponder data. You can see their callsigns and aircraft type!

---

## 4. Pro Tips for Newbies

*   **Wait for the AI:** The platform relies heavily on live AI processing. When you request a Country Brief or a Trade Signal, you will see an "Analyzing..." animation. Be patient for 5-10 seconds while the AI reads the global context.
*   **Don't Panic at the Red Glow:** When a "CRITICAL" intelligence event breaks, the interface might flash with red alert borders (Surge Mode). This just means a high-priority event (like a major earthquake or conflict escalation) was just intercepted by the feed.
*   **Click Everything!** The UI is designed to be interactive. Try clicking on individual news items in the Pulse Feed, or selecting different categories on the top menu to discover new analytical tools.
